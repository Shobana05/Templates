Library Services: Enhancing Your Reading Experience

  1. Online Catalog Access:

    • Explore our vast collection with ease through our user-friendly online catalog.
    • Search by author, title, genre, or keyword to find exactly what you're looking for.
    • Accessible 24/7 for convenience, from any device with an internet connection.

  2. E-Book and Audiobook Downloads:

    • Discover the world of literature on-the-go with our extensive collection of e-books and audiobooks.
    • Enjoy reading or listening from your smartphone, tablet1, or e-reader, anytime and anywhere.
    • Never worry about overdue fines for digital materials.
    • Enjoy automatic returns for digital materials, avoiding overdue fines.

  3. Research Assistance:

    • Our knowledgeable staff is here to assist you with your research needs, whether you're a student, scholar, or enthusiast.
    • Get help locating reliable sources, navigating databases, and refining your search queries.
    • Access premium research tools and databases for in-depth exploration of any topic.

  4. Events and Workshops:

    • Engage with fellow book lovers through our diverse range of events and workshops.
    • Attend author readings, book clubs, writing workshops, and more to connect with the literary community.
    • Expand your horizons with discussions on current topics, literary trends3, and cultural influences.

  5. Children's Programs:

    • Foster a love of reading and learning in children with our engaging programs and activities.
    • Storytime sessions, arts and crafts workshops, and educational events cater to children of all ages.
    • Instill a lifelong appreciation for literature and creativity from an early age.
